Welcome

Welcome to the Sustainable Resource Management System. This platform is designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize the consumption of vital resources within our smart city infrastructure. Our goal is to ensure long-term sustainability through data-driven insights and intelligent resource allocation.

Managed Resources

Water

Monitoring municipal water usage and ensuring efficient distribution to reduce waste.

Energy

Tracking solar and coal energy consumption to promote renewable energy adoption.

Waste

Managing recyclable and non-recyclable waste streams for a cleaner environment.

Sustainability Goals & Analytics

Our system provides real-time analytics to track progress towards our sustainability goals. We monitor key metrics such as:

  • Renewable vs. Non-Renewable Energy Usage: Aiming to maximize solar adoption.
  • Resource Efficiency: Detecting leaks and high-consumption consumers.
  • Carbon Footprint: Estimating environmental impact using external climate data.
